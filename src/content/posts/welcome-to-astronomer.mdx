AstroNomer starts from the spec-first architecture instead of a visual theme first. That gives us a cleaner foundation for multi-instance publishing, host-site mounting, and deploy portability.

  lang="bash"

The first implementation slice already separates:

- environment-driven domain settings
- instance-level operational choices
- design tokens
